The Problem

Generic courses miss the reason you are learning.

A fixed course cannot fit every learner. A designer, an engineer, a student, and a new arrival in Japan do not need the same Japanese.

That is why BES begins with your goals, your real-life situations, and the kind of support you actually need. Then we connect you with the right course, teacher or tutor, and learning plan.

The BES Way

Personalized learning with the right support.

BES was built from cross-cultural language learning and real experience of life in Japan.
At BES, your course is built around:

  • Your goals & interests
  • Your real-life needs
  • Personalized materials you can actually use
  • Teacher or tutor support that fits your learning style
  • A community that keeps learning active between sessions

BES helps you build Japanese you can actually use — with the context and support to find your way in.
